Responsive Website Design/Redesign

Why create responsive and accessible websites? Because the experience matters. Visitors on your site will have a better experience, regardless of browsing device, accesibility needs or celurlar data limits.

I am able to write your website in HTML, CSS and frameworks like BootStrap. In the planning stages, attention is paid to build the website in a uniform structure and style. This aproach allows for efficient modification of the code. The websites can be developed based on a prototype you provide or one that JHC creates for you.

UX & UI Design
